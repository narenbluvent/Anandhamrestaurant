About Anandham Restaurant

Our Story

Welcome to Anandham Restaurant, where every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and a taste of home. Established with a vision to bring authentic South Indian and multi-cuisine flavors to your table, we pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that good food is not just about taste, but about the joy it brings and the memories it creates. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a beloved culinary destination, known for our warm hospitality and commitment to excellence.

We invite you to experience the rich tapestry of flavors, from our aromatic biryanis to our crispy dosas and refreshing milkshakes. At Anandham, we don't just serve food; we serve happiness, one plate at a time.

Meet Our Talented Chefs

Chef Ravi Kumar

With over 20 years of experience, Chef Ravi is our master of traditional South Indian cuisine. His expertise in authentic curries and dosas brings a true taste of home to every dish.

Chef Priya Sharma

Chef Priya specializes in our delectable Biryani and North Indian delicacies. Her innovative approach and meticulous attention to spices create unforgettable flavors.

Chef Arjun Reddy

Our expert in Chinese and Continental fusion, Chef Arjun brings a global touch to our menu. His crispy starters and flavorful noodles are a must-try!

Chef Meena Devi

Chef Meena is our sweet specialist, crafting all our fresh juices, milkshakes, and desserts with a magical touch. Her creations are the perfect end to any meal.
